# 📊 Customer Behaviour Analysis

## 📌 Overview
This project analyzes customer behavior data to uncover patterns in purchasing, subscriptions, and sales performance. The objective is to generate actionable insights that can help businesses improve customer retention and optimize revenue strategies.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Understand customer purchasing patterns  
- Analyze revenue and sales by product category  
- Identify customer distribution across age groups  
- Evaluate subscription behavior  
- Support data-driven decision making  

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- **Python** (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)  
- **SQL** (Data extraction and querying)  
- **Power BI** (Interactive dashboard)  

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- **Total Customers:** 3.9K+  
- **Average Purchase Amount:** $59.76  
- **Average Rating:** 3.75  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- 73% of customers are **non-subscribers**, indicating growth opportunity  
- **Clothing category** generates the highest revenue and sales  
- **Young adults** contribute the most to overall sales  
- Revenue and sales vary significantly across categories and age groups
